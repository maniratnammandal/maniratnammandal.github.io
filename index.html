I recently earned my PhD in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Texas at Austin, where I worked with Prof. Alan Bovik at the Laboratory for Image and Video Engineering (LIVE) Lab. My research focused on building large-scale datasets and deep learning models to assess user-generated image and video quality, collaborating with leading companies like Meta AI, YouTube, and Samsung Research. I graduated from Indian Institue of Technology Kanpur, India with B. Tech. (2018) and M. Tech. (2019) in Electrical Engineering.

I enjoy discussing technology, especially in Media and Entertainment. Beyond work, I love conversations on cinematography, travel, and cuisine. While at home, you can fine me obsessing over gaming technologies or football.
